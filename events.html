Our Events

At AMAL Club, we believe in learning through experience, collaboration, and real-world challenges. Our events are designed to enhance leadership, management, and communication skills, empowering students to step into roles that shape the future. From intellectually stimulating debates to hands-on entrepreneurship workshops, our events provide the perfect platform for students to develop strategic thinking, teamwork, and professional excellence.

Innovate X is AMAL's premier entrepreneurial challenge where participants step into the shoes of startup founders and business strategists. This 2 day workshop encourages students to ideate, develop, and pitch their innovative business solutions to a panel of industry experts and investors.

Mock Parliament

The AMAL Mock Parliament is a flagship event designed to simulate the workings of the Indian Parliament, allowing students to experience real policymaking, debate, and leadership in action. Participants take on roles such as MPs, the Speaker, Prime Minister, and the Leader of Opposition, engaging in heated debates, bill drafting, and voting procedures.

linkedin Optimization

The LinkedIn Optimization Workshop by AMAL Club is designed to help students build a strong professional presence, network effectively, and unlock career opportunities. Whether you're new to LinkedIn or looking to enhance your personal brand, this workshop will provide insider strategies to make your profile stand out!
