About Us

The Amrita Management and Leadership (AMAL) Club at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru, is a vibrant student-driven initiative dedicated to nurturing leadership, management, and communication skills. Our mission is to provide students with a dynamic platform to explore real-world challenges, engage in strategic thinking, and develop the confidence to lead in their future careers

At AMAL, we believe that leadership is not just a title, but a mindset

Our Mission

To provide a platform that nurtures personal and professional development through peer collaboration, skill-building experiences expert-led initiatives focused on management, leadership, and communication.

Our Vision

To be a leading club which empowers students to unlock their full potential and thrive in both personal and professional spheres through collaborative learning experiences.

Core Values

  • Value Creation - Creating value for Amrita students through collaborative efforts
  • Continuous Growth - Growth as individuals, as professionals and as a team
  • Collaborative Leadership
  • Kindness - Kindness towards self and others
  • Innovation & Adaptability
